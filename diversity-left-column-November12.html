Research Opportuni​ties

NIH/NIDCR is dedicated to building an inclusive and diverse community in its training and employment programs.

Individuals from underrepresented ​racial and ethnic groups, individuals with disabilities, and women are always encouraged to apply for NIH/NIDCR support. The following programs may be of particular interest.

​Research Training Sponsored by NIDCR

  • Predoctoral: Ruth L. Kirchenstein National Research Service Awards
  • Principal Investigators: Research Supplements to Promote Diversity in Health-Related Research and Academic Research Enhancement Awards

Research at NIH/NIDCR

  • Students: Undergraduate Scholarship Program and Summer Internships for high school students and college students, dental and medical students, and graduate students​.
  • Recent College Grads: NIH Academy promotes studies of health disparities.
  • Scientists: The Stadtman Tenure-Track Investigators Program
 

NIH​-Wide Diversity

  • Office of Equity, Diversity and Inclusion
  • Office of Scientific Workforce Diversity​
  • Common Fund “Enhancing the Diversity of the NIH-Funded Workforce" 
  • NIH Advisory Committee to the Director Working Group on Diversity
