NIDCR's National Dental Practice-Based Research Network (NDPBRN), headquartered at the University of Alabama at Birmingham, includes six regional nodes.

Click on a nearby regional node for information on how to sign up if you are a dental professional interested in participating in NDPBRN clinical studies. If you are in one of the Federal Dental Services, click on the Birmingham hub/node.
